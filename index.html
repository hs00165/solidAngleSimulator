Solid Angle Simulator

Harrison Sims

A Monte Carlo simulator to calculate the solid angle of a detector relative to a source

Step 1: Define the shape, size and position of the detector relative to the source at the origin.


Step 2: Enter the number of particles to be isotropically emitted from the source between each plot update, and the number of times the plots are updated.


Solid angle: +/- sr
